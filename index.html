Our Services

We deliver exceptional residential and commercial flooring services across the expansive Treasure Valley.

CARPET

Step into our showroom and experience firsthand the limitless possibilities that come from choosing the perfect carpet for your home. With a variety of products designed to meet every individual's style preference, our showroom is designed to help immerse you in the quality, comfort, and beauty of a carpet choice that will enhance the décor of any room in your home.

HARDWOOD

Embark on a sensory journey in our showroom where you can witness the unparalleled quality of our engineered hardwood floors. Seeing is believing, and our exquisite finishes deserve to be experienced firsthand. Step into a world of craftsmanship and sophistication as you explore the rich textures and intricate details that define our hardwood collection. Our showroom is the perfect place to truly appreciate the superior finish that transforms ordinary floors into timeless works of art.

LAMINATE

Today's laminate floors offer wood looks that emulate the warmth and beauty of hardwood floors. Thanks to modern technologies that produce extraordinary embossing and texturing, you’ll have a hard time believing it's not real hardwood. Its strength, durability, and beauty are just a few of the many reasons why so many buyers choose to install laminate flooring in their homes.

LUXURY VINYL PLANK

A flooring option that combines elegance, durability, and affordability brings us to luxury vinyl plank flooring. LVP offers numerous benefits for homeowners, with its water-resistant qualities and its ability to resist scratching—making it a family- and pet-friendly product for any room in your home. Its nonporous surface allows for easy cleanup of spills and messes, saving you time and effort.

TILE / LUXURY VINYL TILE

From the timeless elegance of traditional tile to the contemporary versatility of luxury vinyl tile, our showroom invites you to explore both. Come feel the textures and appreciate the craftsmanship as you explore these options and imagine the possibilities to transform your functional living spaces into your favorite places in the house!

AREA RUGS

Elevate your space with our exquisite selection of stylish area rugs, or create a custom rug in a specific size and shape based on your personal style. Designed to complement any décor, our diverse range of designs ensures there's something for everyone. Crafted with quality materials, our rugs offer durability and softness underfoot—providing both comfort and protection for your floors. Transform any room into a cozy retreat with our stunning selection and redefine elegance in your home with our premium area rugs.
